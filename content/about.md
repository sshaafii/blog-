## About Me

Welcome! I'm Shaafi, and this is my personal blog documenting my journey from Quality Assurance to DevOps and Site Reliability Engineering.

### My Journey

My career began in Quality Assurance, where I developed a deep appreciation for automation, testing methodologies, and ensuring software reliability. Over time, I discovered that my passion extended beyond testing into the broader realm of infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and system reliability.

This transition led me to explore DevOps practices and eventually Site Reliability Engineering, where I could combine my QA background with modern cloud-native technologies and infrastructure automation.

### Skills & Technologies

- **Container Orchestration**: Kubernetes, Docker
- **Linux Systems Administration**: System configuration, performance tuning, troubleshooting
- **DevOps Tools & Automation**: CI/CD pipelines, Infrastructure as Code, configuration management
- **Quality Assurance & Testing**: Test automation, performance testing, reliability engineering
- **Cloud Platforms**: AWS, Azure, GCP
- **Monitoring & Observability**: Prometheus, Grafana, logging solutions

### What You'll Find Here

This blog focuses on practical insights, tutorials, and lessons learned from my journey in DevOps and SRE. Topics include:

- Kubernetes deployment patterns and best practices
- DevOps tooling and automation techniques
- SRE principles and practices
- Career transition insights from QA to DevOps
